About Dr. Prasad Biware

Where Compassion Meets Expertise

Welcome to the official website of Dr. Prasad Biware, a distinguished physician with an unwavering commitment to providing exceptional healthcare services. With a career spanning over two decades, Dr. Biware has established himself as a leading expert in Internal Medicine, earning the trust and admiration of countless patients.Dr. Prasad Biware embarked on his journey in the field of Internal Medicine in 2003. His dedication and vision led him to establish the renowned Shatayushi Hospital in Majalgaon taluka, Beed district. This 100-bed hospital, equipped with a state-of-the-art 10-bed ICU, revolutionized healthcare in the region by offering modern-day facilities previously inaccessible to the local community.

Expertise

Achievements and Healthcare

Unparalleled Initiatives

Dr. Biware's commitment to holistic patient care extends beyond conventional healthcare. He introduced innovative programs such as the monthly 'Diabetic Club,' benefiting over 5,000 diabetic patients by providing regular health check-ups and care. Additionally, the 'Asthma Club' serves more than 5,000 asthmatic patients, emphasizing his dedication to comprehensive healthcare management. He also worked as a Medical officer at PHC Mendkey District Chandrapur in a remote area providing best healthcare services to the sick and old.

Professional Achievements

Dr. Biware's extensive career includes several notable achievements:

1) Shatashu Hospital,Beed (2003-2018): Successfully operated and managed a 100-bed hospital with a 10-bed ICU, setting a high standard of care.
2) Mangeshkar Multispeciality Hospital, Pune: Served as a consultant physician for five years, showcasing his expertise in Internal Medicine.
3) Manipal Hospital: Currently leads the Department of Internal Medicine at Manipal Hospital, where he continues to provide top-tier medical care.
4)Expertise: With zero mortality, Dr. Biware has a remarkable track record of treating over 1,000 patients with poisoning, snakebites, and drowning.

Community Outreach

Dr. Biware's dedication to community health is exemplified by his role as a Lecturer at ICU SRTR Medical College, Ambejogai, and as a Medical Officer at PHC Mendkey, Chandrapur. Furthermore, he served as the CEO of the Shatayushi Foundation, operating mobile medical units across multiple districts to provide free doorstep services to 543 remote villages in Maharashtra since 2011. The mobile media units are operated in and around Beed, Parbhani, Nanded, Hingoli, Raigad, Satara & Nandurbar districts of Maharashtra.
