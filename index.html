About Doctor

  • Dr. Prasad Biware is a well-known and reputed physician working in the field of Internal Medicine since 2003. He successfully established and operated a 100-bed Shatayushi Hospital with 10 bed ICU, the first well-equipped hospital to have provided modern-day health facilities to the people of Majalgaon taluka in Beed district.
  • Apart from just walk-in health facilities he also conducted helpful and unique initiatives like a monthly 'Diabetic Club' providing diabetic patients regular health care, and check-ups, with over 5,000 beneficiaries.
  • He has rendered his yeoman services in the capacity of consultant physician for 5 years at Deenanath Mangeshkar Super speciality Hospital, Pune.
  • He has been passionate about toxicology cases and has successfully treated over 1000 patients with poisoning, snake bites, and drowning with zero death rate.
  • In a special collaboration with the Government of Maharashtra, he successfully operated 10 Mobile Medical Units under his supervision and guidance, providing free preventive, diagnostic, curative and referral services to poor and needy patients from 574 remote travels and underserved villages in Maharashtra since 2011.
